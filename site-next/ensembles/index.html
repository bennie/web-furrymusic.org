Marching Band

An open ensemble accepting of all levels of experience. We try to staff as many conventions that want a pep or marching band. The Anthrocon Fursuit Parade is our largest annual event.

Furry Choir

An open ensemble of all levels of experience. We do one rehearsal and then put on a short performance. We try to perform at as many conventions as possible.

NFC Opera

A full-staged opera. As seen on "Spain's Got Talent!" Soon to enter their third performance season.

Orchestra

A larger concert ensemble path for strings, winds, brass, percussion, and ambitious arrangements.

Big Band

Formerly the Furry Jazz Experience. A full-size big band with singers that performs annually at Anthrocon.

Disco Band

Dance-focused sets, groove, and theatrical performance energy.
